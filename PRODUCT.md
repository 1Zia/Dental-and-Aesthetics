# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Patients in Islamabad seeking premium dental care, facial aesthetics, skin, hair, laser, Botox, fillers, PRP, and natural-looking cosmetic treatments. They may be comparing clinics quietly, looking for a female doctor-led practice, checking trust signals, or trying to book an appointment without a long phone call.

## Product Purpose

Dental & Aesthetic Hub by Dr. Romana is a public marketing and appointment-booking website for a high-end medical-aesthetic clinic in B-17, Islamabad. Success means visitors quickly understand the clinic's dual dental and aesthetic expertise, feel clinical confidence and privacy, and contact the clinic through WhatsApp, phone, or the appointment form.

## Brand Personality

Calm, clinical, composed. The brand should feel like a private medical-aesthetic practice: precise, warm, discreet, and polished without drifting into beauty-salon styling.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic AI landing pages, childish pink beauty themes, loud salon graphics, random blobs, heavy gradients, emoji icons, exaggerated "best clinic" claims, cheap stock-photo collages, repeated icon-card grids, and default shadcn styling.

## Design Principles

- Clinical trust first: every section should reinforce medical expertise, hygiene, privacy, and measured outcomes.
- Soft luxury without noise: use calm spacing, confident typography, refined color, and restrained motion.
- Make booking effortless: WhatsApp, phone, timing, address, and form access should stay close at hand.
- Show breadth without dilution: dental, skin, hair, laser, injectables, and PRP should feel unified under one expert practice.
- Natural outcomes over hype: copy should promise considered care, not miracle transformations.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA. Preserve strong text contrast, keyboard-accessible navigation and forms, clear focus states, descriptive alt text, reduced-motion fallbacks, and mobile-first layouts for patients booking from phones.
